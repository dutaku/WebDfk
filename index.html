Synopsys Program

The program's ultimate goal is to produce graduates who are well-versed in cloud computing. (Online System Hosting). In keeping with the progress of the Industry 4.0 revolution, this sector is one that offers job prospects and is in great demand from businesses. Students will gain practical experience in cloud computing administration and gain exposure to a range of concepts in fibre optics and network computer systems, including virtualization, disaster recovery, and security.
